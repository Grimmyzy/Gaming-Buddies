Gaming Buddies was created with the idea of bringing Gamers together.

A lot of people nowadays play Competetive multiplayer games and more often than not end up not having anyone to play with.

Gaming Buddies main focus is to create a community of Gamers from all over the world and enable people across the globe to enjoy playing their favourite games together with various different players.
