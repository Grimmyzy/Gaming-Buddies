For Honor

For Honor is a Ubisoft game which introduces high dynamic medival combat. There are 5 main fractions in the game. Each fraction has characters representing their relevant timeline for example in the fraction of Vikings you would find characters that would resemble different types of warriors from the Viking times. You also have Samurai and Wu Lin which represent ancient Japanese and Chinese fighters. Each characters comes with a different weapon and playstyle. And requires you to learn the movement and attack combinations.

For Honor comes with a unique play style unline many other games of the similar genre. You are able to deflect, parry or couter your enemys attacks but only if you time and aim your attack the correct way. Once you lock onto an enemy during combat on your screen you will see three curved lines. One on the right side, one on the left side and one on the top side. These represent the directions in which you may attack but also defend. When an enemy that you are locked onto attacks you a red flashing line will appear from the direction that attack is coming from. In that time you can either dodge the attack, deflect it or parry it. Deflecting and parrying the attacks will take quite a bit of practice but it will greatly benefit you and make the gaming experience far more competetive.

League of Legends

League of Legends is a MOBA(multiplayer online battle arena) type of game made by Riot in which you are the Summoner and each match you select a champion that you wish to play with. There are 159 different champions that you can choose from each having 4 different abilities and a passive.

There are various different game modes that you can choose to play with some special ones appearing during events.You can choose to play 5v5 on a wide three lane map with epic monsters hiding in the jungle from which you receive gold and special effects. You can also choose to play 5v5 on a single lane map and enjoy the all out warfare that single lane combat brings.

In addition to the standard modes there are two different Ranked type modes. One reffered to as Ranked Flex and another which is the Ranked solo/duo game. You can choose whether you wish to proceed through the ranks alone or with a single friend and play Ranked Solo/Duo or build a team of 5 and compete against other pre-made teams in Ranked Flex.

And last but not least. At certain periods of time there are events available in which different game modes may occur.

URF for example or Ultra rapid fire is a game mode in which all of your champions abilities cooldown is reduced to a point where you can use ur abilities endlessly and not have to worry about conserving your action points or mana as they are unlimited.

It is a very fun game mode as players are more encouraged into early combat and thus you get to enjoy each match far more.

Rogue Company

Rogue Company is a Hi-Rez Studios game and what you would call a champion shooter. You select a specialist for each match, each specialist comes with an active ability and a passive ability.

There are six main classes that you can choose from in Rogue Company.

  • Defender - A class whos ability focuses on covering teammates or defending objectives.
  • Breacher - The breachers main focus is to push into enemy objectives, breachers often have high area of effect abilities and more resistance to damage.
  • Duelist - These characters specialize in combat. Their abilities are often used to prepare traps or distractions for the enemy team.
  • Intel - Intel characters use abilities such as movement detection devices or sonars to secure your position and have more overall vision and awareness of your enemys presence.
  • Sniper - Sniper class characters use sniper rifles and abilities that expose enemies to them but also provide cover for teammates eg.. smoke bombs or EMP granades.
  • Support - Support characters main purpose is to provide their teamates with usefull effects or simply heal received damage.

Now that you know a little bit about the characters here is some information about the game, Rogue Company is a champion shooter game. There are different game modes available to play.

  • Strikeout - 4v4 Team deathmatch with limited lives per team. ELiminate the enemy team until they run out of lives to win the match.
  • Demolition - 4v4 Objective game mode where one team has a bomb which they need to plant and the other team has to do their best to defend it. There are two objectives in this game mode A and B and either one can be choose to plan the bomb. Half of the match is played as an Attacker (Your team has the bomb) and the other half as a Defender (Your team needs to prevent the enemy team from planting the bomb)
  • Extraction - This is an objective based team where both teams are trying to activate a device and maintain it before the other team takes over or simply wipe out the enemy team. There is only 1 life per round in this game mode so victory can be achieved both ways.
  • Wingman - 2v2 Game mode where you and one more buddy can play against another duo. This game mode was created to allow people to enjoy a casual 2v2 shoot out with a friend.
